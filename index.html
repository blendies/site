About Us

Welcome to Blendies!
Bound by a passion for creativity and trying new things, a group of friends decided to share our journey with the world. We specialise in offering a diverse range of beautifully handcrafted items, from digital art and pyrography to air-dry clay and acrylic paints. Our mission is to connect with anyone interested in exploring their artistic side and discovering the joy of craftsmanship. By creating a space where creativity thrives, we aim to build a welcoming community where everyone can find something new to try, a source of inspiration in our busy lives and even friendship along the way!
